work simulator

Its 6:00am and its time for work, you get ready and leave the
house and you have a choice for taking the bus or the train?

You get to the bus stop and you see two buses a green one and a yellow one, which one will you take?

The driver turns on to a road with loads of traffic and the driver is
offering people if they want to walk instead, Will you walk or stay on the bus?

You get to work about a minute late and your boss is shouting and screaming at you and he also docks your wages for a month,
you get this urge to punch him, do you punch him or try to pull back?

You finish work and its time to go home, What do you take? bus or train?
